Introduction

In the summer of 2010, Daniel Moore and I worked on a project in algebraic geometry with Ursula Whitcher . A major part of the project was to develop computational techniques for computing properties of K3 surfaces. The other part of the project was to work with a special family of K3 surfaces whose corresponding polytope has an S4 symmetry. For this family, we used Griffith-Dwork algorithm to compute the Picard-Fuchs differential equations. The paper is available at ArXiv. A poster for presentation is available here.

Code
